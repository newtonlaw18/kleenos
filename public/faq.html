Frequetly Asked Questions

  • Business
  • Residents
How do I place an order?
You may place an order by going to 'Get A Quote' page and submit the form or simply drop us an email at support@kleenos.com .
Can I reschedule my pickup or delivery?
Absolutely. Pick-up and delivery time for businesses are prearranged as per agreements, weekly on a scheduled day and time. However if you wish to reschedule your pickup or delivery, just give us a call. Please note that all changes to pickup or delivery must be made 24 hours before the agreed scheduled time. 
Is there a minimum order?
The minimum order is RM65.
Do I get a receipt?
We will provide you with an invoice once your order has been fully completed and delivered to you. You may also ask for an electronic invoice copy sent to your email address. Please double check your email account, including your junk or spam folder. Note that emails can take up to 24 hours. 
Who do I contact in case of changes or other related concerns?
You will be assigned to a point-of-contact person (POC), base on your contract. For any related issues or concern with your laundry services you may contact your POC.
How will I know how much my order weighs?
Total weight of item(s) will be confirmed once it arrives our facility. That amount will be reflected in your invoice.
How do I pay?
At the moment we accept cash payments only. Payments will be collected after every delivery. Please refer to your contract PRICING section for more information.
