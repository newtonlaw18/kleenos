About Us

Kleenos started operations in 2018 under Triple L Industry PLT. Kleenos started as an idea to allow convenient access and affordable laundry services to users everywhere. The idea came about because during the rising age of technology, various type of services are being offered to facilitate users daily activities. One of our partners thought of implementing a laundry and dry cleaning service to reduce heavy work-load and give users more time to do their own things.

"No matter how hard I try to keep up with it there is always a mountain of laundry. I'm starting to think it's self-breeding. That's why I started Kleenos."

-Frederick, Co-founder of Kleenos
